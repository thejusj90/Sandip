How the work unfolds

A progression, not a technique.

01

Understand

We begin with how you currently breathe — not just in the room, but across your day.

02

Identify

We notice the patterns that appear repeatedly, and which ones are just noise.

03

Retrain

We work intentionally with more useful patterns, gradually and practically.

04

Integrate

The goal is not to think about breathing all day. It is to help a different pattern become more familiar.
