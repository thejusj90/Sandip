What to expect

Every consultation starts with understanding your everyday breathing pattern — not just how you breathe in the room with Sandip, but how your breathing likely shows up across your day: at work, under stress, while you sleep.

From there, the work is personalised: identifying the specific habits worth addressing, and working gradually to retrain them so the change holds on its own.
